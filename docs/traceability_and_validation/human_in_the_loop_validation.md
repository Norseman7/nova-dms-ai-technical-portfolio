# Human In The Loop Validation

## Principle

Human review is a required safeguard across NOVA, DMS, and the Figma workflow. Automation may propose, measure, and summarize, but trusted acceptance remains human-controlled.

## Human Review Points

| Area | Human role |
| --- | --- |
| NOVA event modules | Approve definitions, thresholds, validation examples, and accepted module versions. |
| NOVA outcomes | Review timestamped evidence, validity, and sample-size interpretation. |
| Brain Store | Approve promotion from generated evidence to trusted evidence. |
| AI reasoning | Review generated explanations before operational use. |
| DMS audits | Review findings, deferred checks, and remediation scope. |
| Figma workflow | Approve selected scope, previewed changes, and final design state. |

## Why It Matters

Human-in-the-loop validation protects against look-ahead errors, weak samples, overconfident AI explanations, UI drift, and architecture changes that are technically plausible but operationally wrong.
